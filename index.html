About Apple

Apple Inc. is a global leader in technology, known for its innovative hardware, software, and services. Founded in 1976 by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne, Apple has revolutionized the consumer electronics industry with iconic products like the iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Apple Watch. The company is also renowned for its software platforms, including iOS, macOS, watchOS, and tvOS, which seamlessly integrate across devices to provide a cohesive user experience. In addition to its hardware and software offerings, Apple provides a wide range of services, such as the App Store, Apple Music, Apple Pay, and iCloud, all designed to enhance productivity, creativity, and entertainment. Apple is committed to sustainability, accessibility, and privacy, driving innovation while ensuring a positive impact on the world. Headquartered in Cupertino, California, Apple continues to shape the future of technology with a relentless focus on quality, design, and user satisfaction.
